About EcoSpace

We will need to carry plants into space when humans travel there for both practical and horticultural reasons. Fresh flowers and gardens on the International Space Station provide a lovely ambience and allow us to bring a small portion of Earth with us on our adventures, as we already know from our pioneering astronauts. Both here on Earth and in space, they are beneficial to our psychological health. Additionally, they will be essential for maintaining astronauts' health throughout lengthy trips.
